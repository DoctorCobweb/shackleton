Tiklet.me ticketing services

Tiklet.me is a digital ticketing service. It delivers tickets directly to your email address and also to any iPhone user's (running iOS 6+) Passbook application. Signup is free and we don't onsell any of your private details. All we do is tickets.

The future of ticketing is integrating your mobile device to house all your ticketing needs. From searching for tickets, purchasing them easily and storing them securely directly on your mobile, Tiklet.me aims to make the feeling of buying your tickets easy, intuitive and modern.

Tiklet.me is also built with the environment in mind; for a paperless future where technology helps us all to replace obsolete & inefficient practices.
